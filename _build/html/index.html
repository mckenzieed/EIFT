News-api Reference¶

eift_core.news_api.news_api.get_news_articles_everything(api_key, keyword=None, sources=None, domains=None, date_from=None, date_to=None, language='en', sort_by='popularity', page_size=100, page=1)¶

Gets all news articles based on the arguments provided.

Parameters:
  • api_key – Required to query.
  • keyword – Used to determine keywords to look for in articles.
  • sources – Only bring back articles with sources that are in this list.
  • domains – Only bring back articles with sources that have domains in this list.
  • date_from – Articles that were written on or after this date are retrieved.
  • date_to – Articles that were written on or before this date are retrieved.
  • language – Only bring back articles defined with this language.
  • sort_by – How articles are sorted. (relevancy, popularity, publishedAt)
  • page_size – How many pages of articles to bring back. Default is 20, max is 100.
  • page – Which page of the page_size pages to bring back.
Returns:

List of articles retrieved in JSON form.
eift_core.news_api.news_api.get_news_articles_top_headlines(api_key, country=None, category=None, sources=None, keyword=None, page_size=100, page=1)¶

Gets all news articles based on the arguments provided.

Parameters:
  • api_key – Required to query.
  • country – Country the articles were published in. Cannot be used if ‘sources’ is used.
  • category – The category to get headlines for. Choices are ‘business’, ‘entertainment’, ‘general’, ‘health’, ‘science’, ‘sports’, ‘technology’ Cannot be used if ‘sources’ is used.
  • sources – Articles written by these sources are brought back. Cannot be used with ‘category’ or ‘country’.
  • keyword – Bring back articles containing this keyword.
  • page_size – How many pages of articles to bring back. Default is 20, max is 100.
  • page – Which page of the page_size pages to bring back.
Returns:

List of articles retrieved in JSON form.
